PCC Thrift Instructions

Welcome to PCC Thrift! This page provides instructions on how to access and use the PCC Thrift Marketplace. Please read the appropriate section based on your status.

Students and Faculty

To access the project, you will log into Eagles Nest with the link below. For faculty members, you will have your own account only if you have the faculty extension on your email (i.e. yourname@faculty.pcci.edu). The link to the PCC Thrift Marketplace will be on Eagles Nest with a large colorful button named "Marketplace", use this to access the project!

Others

If you do not have an Eagles Nest login, please move to the designated guest computers that will have guest access to the marketplace ( Expo staff is willing to assist! :D ) If you are already sitting there, then you can proceed with the link below.
